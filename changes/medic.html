Medic

Syringe Gun

On top of being massively overshadowed by the Crossbow, the Syringe Gun archetype suffers from slow projectile speed and other jank that makes it unnessesarily clunky to use.

I'd also like to take this opportunity to rework Medic, as he's a design I've never really liked. Few people particularly like playing him, and yet he's powerful to the degree that not having him on your team is throwing, which only exacerbates the previous issue. I think the first problem is because Medic is overly passive -- his healing requires minimal active input, he's actively discouraged from participating in combat, and he has little ability to make plays on his own. Shifiting some focus onto the primary weapon both as a weapon and a medical tool could help with this.

  • High-level goal: Make the Medic more active, and encourage flow between healing and hurting
  • Low-level goal: Make the Syringe Gun a more satisfying weapon to use
  • Changes:
    • (+) Alt-fire: Fire a syringe that heals 25-50 health on hit (inversely proportional to distance) and applies a 2 second 25 HP/s regeneration buff
    • The alt-fire requires and consumes an entire clip
  • (+) This weapon reloads while not active
  • (+) Max damage ramp-up reverted to 150%
  • (+) Projectile velocity increased to 1300 HU/s
  • (+) Syringes no longer have projectile deviation
  • (-) Clip reduced to 30

Crusader's Crossbow

The Syringe Gun changes demand that the Crossbow's identity be re-examined. Instead of damage for healing, I want the trade-off to be short-range self-defence for mid-range offensive burst. With neither weapon being obviously better at doing Medic things, I hope there isn't any obligation to pick one or the other.

  • Goal: Bring the Crossbow to the level of the new Syringe Gun
  • Changes:
    • (+) On hit: Reveals enemy health to teammates for 2 seconds
    • The alt-fire requires and consumes an entire clip
    • (-) Healing reduced to 25-50 health on hit (inversely proportional to distance) with a 2 second 25 HP/s regeneration buff
    • (-) Max ammo reduced to 10

Crusader's Crossbow

Crossbow

Fires special bolts that heal teammates and deals damage based on distance traveled.

This weapon will reload automatically when not active.

Medi-Gun

Given the changes to the Syringe Gun, I can't just leave the Medi-Gun as it is. I want to reduce its output to compensate for the primary healing, so the Medic isn't putting out too much healing overall. The ability to keep multiple people overhealed at a time also outdoes what most classes are able to bring to their team, and I think restricting overheal mainly to one person at a time would be

  • Goal: Make the Medic more active, and encourage flow between healing and hurting
  • Changes:
    • (-) Heal rate reduced to 18 HP/sec (was 24)
    • (-) Overheal depletes three times as fast when not actively being healed
