Dead Ringer

Iconic though it may be, the Dead Ringer's feign is ineffective. On top of the technical issues that sometimes give it away, once opponents catch on to the Spy's choice of watch, they can just assume any death is a feign and usually be correct. This is why the Dead Ringer grants resistance and speed. The point is the free disengage rather than the deception. If Dead Ringer Spies could cloak into the backlines like regular Spies and the feign was a secondary ability, my hope is that it would become less predictable and therefore the actual deception would be stronger.
