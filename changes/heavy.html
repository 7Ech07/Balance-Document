Heavy

Minigun

Heavy is one of the least-played classes in the game. I suspect the two main reasons for this are the lack of playstyle diversity, and the lack of dynamic gameplay. Playstyle diversity will mostly be handled by unlock changes, but I think making the Shotgun a more relevant part of Heavy gameplay would also help, alongside making Heavy more dynamic and increasing his skill cap.

  • Goal: Bring the Crossbow to the level of the new Syringe Gun
  • Changes:
    • (+) Heavy can now swap weapons while revved
    • (+) Accuracy is no longer reduced for the first second after revving up
    • (+) The Minigun no longer deals reduced damage to leveled Sentry Guns
    • (~) The Minigun's damage is now split between 12 bullets per shot rather than 4
    • The bullets are distributed in a fixed spread pattern as follows -- 1 in the centre, 5 in a small ring around it, and 6 in a larger ring around that
    • (-) Damage lowered by 11% (max of 32 damage per burst, from 36)
