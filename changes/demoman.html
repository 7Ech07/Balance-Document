Stickybomb Launcher

Compared to a lot of other people, I'm mostly fine with the power of Demoman. However, I would like to introduce more counterplay to traps. Currently, traps have few ways to effectively destroy them and aren't telegraphed as well as many of the game's other one-shots.

  • Goal: Make the weapon less clunky
  • Changes:
    • (-) Stickies have a particle effect that shows their arm timer, and flash and beep periodically when armed
    • (-) Stickies are destroyed by explosions if they are within the inner half of the blast radius
    • (-) Stickybomb hitbox dimensions are doubled (8x8x8 instead of 4x4x4)
      • This does not impact projectile physics (the model is used for that); it only makes stickies easier to shoot
