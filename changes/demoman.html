Stickybomb Launcher

Compared to a lot of other people, I'm mostly fine with the power of Demoman. However, I would like to introduce more counterplay to traps. Currently, traps have few ways to effectively destroy them and aren't telegraphed as well as many of the game's other one-shots.

I also think making stickies more obvious in general would make them more intuitive to play against. Rocket visuals make it clear where they are and where they're going, and they read as immediate threats. Stickies aren't as obvious and don't look immediately threatening even though they absolutely are.

  • Goal: Increase counterplay to sticky traps
  • Changes:
    • (-) Stickies have a particle effect that shows their arm timer, and flash and beep periodically when armed
    • (-) Stickies are destroyed by explosions if they are within the inner half of the blast radius
    • (-) Stickybomb hitbox dimensions are doubled (8x8x8 instead of 4x4x4)
      • This does not impact projectile physics (the model is used for that); it only makes stickies easier to shoot

Stickybomb Launcher

Stickybomb Launcher

Fires stickybombs that attach to surfaces. Stickybombs can be destroyed by enemy attacks.

Alt-fire: Detonate all armed stickybombs.

Scottish Resistance

This weapon is very underused because of how specialised, slow and defensive it is. Giving it sticky spam capabilities would run counter to the weapon's design, but I'd like to see if I can give the weapon some offensive capabilities while still feeling like itself.

  • Goal: Make the weapon faster
  • Changes:
    • (+) Charging prepares up to 3 additional stickies at once
    • Each second of charging prepares another sticky, and all prepared stickies are fired at once (similar to the Beggar's Bazooka)
    • (+) This weapon reloads when not active
    • This weapon reloads at half the speed while holstered
    • (-) Removed: Explosions destroy enemy stickybombs (now intrinsic to all explosives)
