Direct Hit

I think the extent to which the Direct Hit counters certain things is excessive. It one-shots Scout even up to the range where Scout needs four shots to kill the Soldier, it deals massive damage to blast jumpers despite being already the easiest launcher to hit airshots with, and it can damage Sentries from infinite range faster than they can be repaired.

However, the Direct Hit isn't exactly overpowered, so I can't just nerf it and leave it like that. I wanted to do something to partially alleviate the weapon's weaknesses without compromising its identity, and make the Mini-Crit more relevant.

  • Goal: Remove the potential for one-shot kills and rebalance the weapon to compensate
  • Changes:
    • (+) Added: On impact, projectiles create a shockwave that pushes enemies away
      • The shockwave is harmless, and has 66% of the radius and push force of a stock rocket's explosion
      • Enemies launched airborne by the shockwave can be Mini-Crit airshot
      • The push force of the shockwave does not stack with the actual damaging explosion (so shooting people in the feet does not send them into space)
    • (-) Only Mini-Crits targets launched by the userâ€™s explosives
    • (-) Ramp-up reduced to 110%
      • Max damage of 124
    • (-) Damage bonus only applies against players

Mantreads

The Mantreads require a lot of setup to use, and even then are less lethal than the Market Gardener. To make them properly worth the secondary slot, I'm adding a new mechanic that should increase the weapon's power, usability and versatility, while also being a lot of fun.

  • Goal: Increase the lethality and useability of the stomp
  • Changes:
    • (+) Added: While airborne, hold jump to perform a slam, increasing your gravity and creating an explosion upon landing
      • Jump must be held for 1 second to activate, and then the slam cannot be canceled out of
      • The explosion deals double your fall damage at base in an area the size of a rocket explosion (which also damages any enemies you land directly on)
      • Increased gravity increases your fall damage, and you can only avoid fall damage when landing on an enemy, so be careful!
    • (-) Knockback resistance only applies when grounded
    • (-) Removed: 300% increased air control on wearer
